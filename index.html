I'm a Sloth.

I am a mammal.

Hello.

I am a Pygmy Three-Toed-Sloth. My scientific name is Bradypus Pygmaeus and I am a herbivore. I live in the tropical rainforests of South and Central America.

My Skills.

Defense

Despite being a slow and otherwise sleepy animal, I can defend myself from vicious predators such as Anacondas and Eagles. My claws and teeth allow me to defend myself and I can turn my 270 degrees to scan for predetors up above and on the ground.

Camoflage

My tan and brownish fur matches that of a tree. This makes me invisible to predators. A green algae also grows on my fur which allows me to blend into the foliage.

Sloth Populations Are Decreasing

Unfortunately, sloth populations are decreasing due to an increase in tropical rain forests being cut down. Sloths lose their homes and become easy prey to predators. To find out how you can help and learn more about sloths, click the button below.
